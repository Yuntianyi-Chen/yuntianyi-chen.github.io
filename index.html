Biography

Yuntianyi Chen is an undergraduate majoring in Computer Science and Technology at Wuhan University. His research interests include software configuration, transfer learning, evolutionary computation and defect prediction. He joined the Centre of Software Testing, Analysis and Reliability (CSTAR) since 2017 under the supervision of professor Jifeng Xuan.
